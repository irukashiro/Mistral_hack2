# Project Description — Class Conflict: Millionaire

---

## 日本語（約500字）

誰もが知るトランプゲーム「大富豪」を、人狼ゲームの心理戦と Mistral AI で再発明したブラウザゲームです。

閉鎖空間に集められた5人の参加者には、「富豪・貧民・平民」という秘密の階級が割り振られます。夜は大富豪でカードを出し合い、そのプレイングが翌日の会議で「あいつが強すぎる、富豪だ！」という証拠になります。昼の会議では議論・投票で危険分子を処刑。カードゲームと人狼が連動した、全く新しい推理体験です。

最大の目玉は、Mistral AI を活用した**陽動イカサマシステム**。貧民は「陽動」と「本命」の2段階アクションを自由なテキストで入力し、ターゲットは突然表示された陽動に5秒以内で反応しなければなりません。言葉による生々しい騙し合いをリアルタイムで実現しました。

NPCは「Trust（論理）× Affinity（感情）」の2軸パラメータで動き、人狼セオリーを自ら導き出しながらも、性格によって非合理な行動をとります。ゲーム終了時には全キャラクターの裏設定・隠れ勝利条件が一斉開示され、強烈な伏線回収体験を提供します。

## English (~500 words)

**Class Conflict: Millionaire** is a browser-based game that reinvents the classic Japanese card game *Daifugo* (Rich Man, Poor Man) by fusing it with social deduction mechanics and Mistral AI-powered characters.

Five players are locked in a closed space and secretly assigned to one of three classes: **Fugo (Rich)**, **Hinmin (Poor)**, or **Heimin (Common)**. Each night, they play Daifugo — but every card played becomes evidence. Strong hands spark suspicion: *"You played a Joker twice last night — you must be the Fugo!"* Each day, players debate, accuse, and vote to execute the most dangerous person. The card table is the crime scene. The council is the courtroom.

The standout feature is the **Decoy Bluff System**, powered by Mistral AI. The Hinmin player crafts a two-part attack: a **Decoy** action to misdirect, and a **Real** action to steal cards. The target has just **5 seconds and 15 characters** to react — only to the Decoy, not knowing the real intent. Human psychology does the rest: panic leads to mistakes, and the attacker wins.

NPC behavior is driven by a dual-axis parameter system — **Trust (logic)** and **Affinity (emotion)** — enabling distinct personality archetypes. A *Logical* NPC rigorously applies werewolf theory (roller tactics, line analysis). An *Emotional* NPC understands the logic but defends a friend anyway: *"I don't care what the detective said — I trust them!"* This human-like irrationality generates emergent drama every session.

At the end of each game, a reveal screen exposes every character's secret backstory, hidden win condition, and relationship map — delivering a powerful "aha moment" as players realize why each NPC acted the way they did. Every playthrough tells a completely different story, driven by Mistral AI.
